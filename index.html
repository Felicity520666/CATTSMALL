Catt Small

One of my role models is Catt Small because she is a game designer that is committed to making the game industry more diversive! She co-founded Brooklyn Gamery which creats diversity-focuused games, and Code Liberation which is a non-profit organization that teaches women, non-binary, femme, and girl-identifying people to program!

Her games can be found on the Brooklyn Gamery website where you can preview and buy her games!
